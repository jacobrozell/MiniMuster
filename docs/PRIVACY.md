# Privacy Policy — MiniMuster (iOS)

**Effective date:** June 15, 2026  
**App:** MiniMuster  
**Bundle ID:** com.jacobrozell.minimuster

## Summary

MiniMuster is a **local-first** hobby tracker. Your armies, units, paints, and settings are stored **on your device**. We do not operate servers that receive your collection data, and we do not sell or share your information.

## Information we collect

**We do not collect personal information from you.**

The app does not require an account, email address, or sign-in.

## Information you store in the app

You may enter hobby-related data, such as:

- Army and unit names, quantities, painting states, notes, and tags  
- Paint inventory (names, brands, types, quantities, sources)  
- App preferences (theme, filters, pipeline stages, faction display overrides)

This data is saved locally using **SwiftData** on your iPhone or iPad.

## Information shared between app components

The optional home-screen **widget** reads a small summary (for example, model counts on the sprue) from an **App Group** shared container on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or third parties.

## Import and export

You may import or export CSV files and JSON backups using the system file picker and share sheet. Those actions are **initiated by you**. The app does not upload imports or exports to our servers.

## Network use

MiniMuster **does not** make network requests to collect, sync, or analyze your data. (Your device’s operating system may perform its own network activity unrelated to this app.)

## Analytics and tracking

We do **not** use analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or cross-app tracking in MiniMuster.

## Children’s privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it (individual items, **Clear all data** in Settings, or uninstalling the app). Uninstalling removes local app data subject to iOS backup behavior you control in system settings.

## Your rights

Because data stays on your device, you control it directly: view, edit, export, or delete at any time through the app or by removing the app.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The **Effective date** at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
